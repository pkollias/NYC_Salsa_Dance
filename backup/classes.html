First Time?

What to Expect

Do I need a partner?

No. We rotate partners throughout. Come alone — you'll dance with everyone in the room.

Which class should I start with?

If you've never danced salsa before, start with Beginners on Tuesday or Thursday. If you have some experience, try Intermediate on Saturday.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothes you can move in. Dance shoes help but aren't required. Bring a change of shirt.

How do I book and pay?

All bookings go through the ForActive app.  Book here →

Is it truly beginner-friendly?

Yes. The community is built around welcoming people who have never danced before. If you've wanted to start, Tuesday or Thursday is your moment.
