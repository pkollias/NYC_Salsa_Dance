Why Here

01

No Experience Needed

The beginner class is built from zero. If you've never danced a step in your life, Tuesday or Thursday night is your starting line.

02

Community Over Performance

This is not a showcase. No auditions, no competitions. Just people who want to move, connect, and have a real night in Brooklyn.

03

Rooted in NYC Salsa

We teach on-2 — the New York style. Grounded in the tradition that made this city the salsa capital of the world.
